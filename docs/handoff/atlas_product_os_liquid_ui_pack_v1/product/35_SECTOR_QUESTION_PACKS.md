# Sector Question Packs

Each sector has a question pack.

Questions must be concrete, one at a time, with choices, examples and validation.

Examples:

Restaurante:
- Do you have a menu? PDF/image/web/Excel/no.
- Do you control ingredients? yes/software/Excel/no/more or less.
- Do you use POS? yes/no/not sure.

Gestoría:
- Do you work with autónomos, companies or both?
- Where are client files stored?
- What accounting software do you use?
- Do you use digital certificate?
