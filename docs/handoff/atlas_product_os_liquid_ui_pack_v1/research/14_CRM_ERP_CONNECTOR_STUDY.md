# CRM / ERP Connector Study

CRM and ERP need domain abstractions, not only generic connectors.

CRM layer maps contacts, companies, leads, deals, stages, activities, meetings, notes, tasks, quotes and contracts.

ERP layer maps customers, suppliers, products, inventory, invoices, bills, receipts, delivery notes, sales orders, purchase orders, payments, taxes and reports.

Writes are gated. Read-only first.
