# Manifest

This ZIP contains the complete consolidated plan from Research 01 to Research 15.

## Major blocks

- `/context` — prior handoff and decisions.
- `/product` — product constitution and product primitives.
- `/research` — research dossiers and reference policies.
- `/sectors` — sector taxonomy and sector specs.
- `/design` — UX, native UI, presence, screens and quality gates.
- `/backend` — architecture contracts and engine specs.
- `/schemas` — JSON schemas for registries, workbenches, integrations and business core.
- `/fixtures` — demo data for Fable tests and scaffolds.
- `/adr` — architecture decision records.
- `/tasks` — execution order, acceptance criteria and prohibitions.
- `/continuation` — templates Fable must keep updated.
